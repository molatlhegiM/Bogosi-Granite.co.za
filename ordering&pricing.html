Pricing & Ordering .

The price of the memorial may differ based on the premium enhancements the customer selected. Always enquire about the specific pricing of the pictured memorial. The prices for products that can be purchased on our brochure are fixed and some will vary according to the customer’s specification.

To place an order, call or email, and let us schedule an appointment. When you are ready to order your memorial, please have the following information handy:
• Deceased Name and date of birth and death
• Cemetery Name, City, and or Province
• Grave location information (section and grave number etc.)
• If you wish to duplicate an existing memorial, a photo of it is very helpful
